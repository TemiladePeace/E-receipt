About Us

What E-receipt is all about?

E-receipt is a fully integrated online platform, offering seamless solutions to all your transaction needs. We aim to support businesses and ensure that receipts are issued in an easier and effective manner. Each receipt is uniquely designed and identifiable. We believe that business growth is hinged on the quality of the connection between business and customers. Suitable for all business owners, Individuals and Organization at large.
E-receipts makes it easier, faster and cheaper for business providers to issue and send receipts to their customer in a convenient, secure manner. It is designed to give you greater control, less stress and increased value for your business. We offer:

  • Convenience
  • Flexibility
  • Reliability.
  • Accessibility
  • Business Growth
